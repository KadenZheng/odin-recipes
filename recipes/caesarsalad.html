Caesar Salad

Ingredients

  • 1 romaine lettuce, chopped
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan
  • 1 cup croutons
  • Caesar dressing (store-bought or homemade)
  • Grilled chicken breast, sliced (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine chopped romaine, Parmesan, and croutons.
  2. Drizzle with Caesar dressing, toss to coat evenly.
  3. Top with grilled chicken slices if using. Serve immediately.

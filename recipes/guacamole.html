Fresh Guacamole

Ingredients

  • 3 ripe avocados
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup diced onion
  • 3 tbsp chopped cilantro
  • 2 Roma tomatoes, diced
  • 1 tsp minced garlic
  • 1 pinch cayenne pepper (optional)

Instructions

  1. Mash avocados in a bowl.
  2. Add lime juice and salt, mix well.
  3. Stir in onion, cilantro, tomatoes, and garlic.
  4. Add cayenne pepper if desired. Serve with tortilla chips.

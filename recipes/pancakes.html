Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 3/4 cup buttermilk
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tbsp melted butter
  • Butter or oil for cooking

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  2. In another bowl, whisk buttermilk, milk, egg, and melted butter.
  3. Combine wet and dry ingredients until just blended.
  4. Heat a griddle or pan over medium heat, greased lightly.
  5. Pour 1/4 cup batter for each pancake. Cook until bubbles form, then flip and cook until golden brown.
  6. Serve with syrup and toppings of choice.

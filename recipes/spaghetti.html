Spaghetti Aglio e Olio

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 3 cloves garlic, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1/4 tsp red pepper flakes
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped
  • Parmesan cheese (optional)

Instructions

  1. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large pan, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add garlic and red pepper flakes, saut√© until garlic is golden.
  3. Add cooked spaghetti to the pan, tossing to coat in oil and garlic.
  4. Season with salt, garnish with parsley and Parmesan if desired. Serve hot.
